Privacy Policy

Fleet Commander

Effective date: 6 March 2026

Fleet Commander is a single-player iOS game. This policy explains what data is handled when you use the app and which Apple platform services are involved.

Data handled by the app

Fleet Commander uses the following categories of data:

  • Gameplay progress, such as saved systems, fleets, research state, prestige progress, and player settings
  • Cosmetic purchase entitlements returned by Apple's App Store infrastructure
  • Optional Game Center status, achievements, and leaderboard submissions when you sign in to Game Center

How data is stored

Gameplay progress is stored locally on your device. The app also attempts to use CloudKit through your private iCloud account so progress can sync across your own devices. When CloudKit is unavailable or not permitted, Fleet Commander falls back to local storage and remains fully playable.

The developer does not operate a separate account system or collect gameplay data on an external backend.

Purchases

Fleet Commander offers optional cosmetic-only in-app purchases. Purchase processing, receipts, and entitlement restoration are handled through Apple. The game does not use purchases to unlock combat power or progression advantages.

Analytics and advertising

Fleet Commander does not include third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers. The app contains no ads.

Game Center

Game Center features are optional. If you sign in, Apple may process your Game Center profile, achievement progress, and leaderboard submissions according to Apple's own privacy terms. Fleet Commander remains playable without Game Center.

Your choices

  • You can play without signing in to Game Center.
  • You can restore cosmetic purchases from the in-game store.
  • You can manage iCloud and Game Center permissions in iOS settings.
  • You can remove local app data by deleting the app from your device.

Support and privacy questions

Support information is available on the Fleet Commander support page. For product issues, bug reports, or privacy questions related to the app's behaviour, use the support routes listed there.

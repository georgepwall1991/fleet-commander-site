Single-Player Galactic Strategy

Command a fleet. Shape a galaxy.

Fleet Commander is a free iPhone strategy game about colonising star systems, building task forces, researching upgrades, and holding the line as hostile pressure spreads across the map. The game includes optional cosmetic-only purchases, optional Game Center features, and cloud sync through Apple services when available.

What the game includes

  • Deterministic galaxy generation with connected star lanes
  • Fleet dispatch, battle resolution, and research progression
  • Campaign pressure systems that force defense and recovery choices
  • Optional Game Center achievements and leaderboard support
  • Cosmetic-only in-app purchases with built-in restore flow

Store model

Fleet Commander is free to download. The in-game store only sells visual cosmetics such as skins, themes, and nebula variants. Purchases never affect combat power, research speed, progression, or matchmaking.

The game contains no ads and no pay-to-win mechanics.

Data and services

Progress is stored on device. When CloudKit is available, the app can sync supported save data through the player's private iCloud account. If CloudKit is unavailable, Fleet Commander falls back to local storage and remains playable.

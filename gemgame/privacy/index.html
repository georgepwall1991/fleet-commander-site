Privacy Policy

GemGame

Effective date: 7 April 2026

GemGame is an iPhone puzzle game. This policy explains what data the app handles, how that data is stored, and which Apple services are involved when you make a purchase.

Data handled by the app

  • Local gameplay progress, including unlocked levels, coins, stars, garden state, and settings
  • Purchase entitlement state returned by Apple's App Store infrastructure for the Hard Mode Pack

How data is stored

GemGame stores gameplay progress and settings locally on your device. The developer does not operate a separate player account system or an external gameplay backend for the app.

Purchases

GemGame offers a single optional non-consumable purchase called the Hard Mode Pack. Purchase processing, receipts, and entitlement restoration are handled by Apple.

Analytics and advertising

GemGame does not include third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers. The app contains no ads.

Your choices

  • You can play without creating an account.
  • You can restore the Hard Mode Pack from the in-game shop if you bought it previously.
  • You can remove local app data by deleting the app from your device.

Support and privacy questions

Support information is available on the GemGame support page. For product issues, bug reports, or privacy questions related to the app's behaviour, use the support route listed there.
